Treatment for HIV

There is no cure for HIV, but it can be controlled with medication. It is treated with antiretroviral drugs, which stop the virus from replicating in the body.


  • Antiretroviral therapy (ART) is the standard treatment.
  • ART helps reduce the viral load in the body.
  • People on ART can live long, healthy lives.
  • Consistent treatment prevents the progression to AIDS.
